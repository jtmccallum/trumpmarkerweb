Trump Marker
Does the question "what's trump again?" come up too often in your euchre games? TrumpMarker helps keep track of this hand's trump suit if you click or tap on a suit after calling.

Click from suit to suit as trump changes, click again on a suit to 'un-trump' it, or use the Clear button to show that trump hasn't been called.
